EMG-Controlled Computer Mouse

Circuit Design

Collaborated with a team of engineering students to design an electromyogram (EMG) signal controlled circuit that operates the left click button on a computer mouse.

Incrementally built and tested the circuit under the supervision of a senior electronics engineer to incorporate suitable signal amplification, rectification, integration, and user protection using appropriate electrical hardware components.
